\subsection{Applications of Least Squares}

Least squares appears everywhere in engineering and science.

%------------------------------------------------
\subsubsection*{1. Linear Regression}

Fit model:

\[
    y = mx + c.
\]

\subsubsection*{2. Polynomial Approximation}

Fit quadratic:

\[
    y = ax^2 + bx + c.
\]

Matrix form:

\[
    A =
    \begin{bmatrix}
        x_1^2  & x_1    & 1      \\
        x_2^2  & x_2    & 1      \\
        \vdots & \vdots & \vdots
    \end{bmatrix}.
\]

%------------------------------------------------
\subsubsection*{3. Signal Processing}

Noise removal via projection.

%------------------------------------------------
\subsubsection*{4. Machine Learning}

Least squares underlies:

\begin{itemize}
    \item Linear regression
    \item Ridge regression
    \item Principal Component Analysis
\end{itemize}

\subsubsection*{Engineering Insight}

\begin{noteBox}
    Least squares converts inconsistent systems
    into optimal approximate solutions
    via orthogonal projection.
